About Zylo

Instruments for sound that refuses to sit still.

Zylo builds focused software instruments and effects for producers, sound designers, and experimental creators who want motion, texture, and atmosphere to feel playable.

Why We Build

Sound design should feel like touching a machine, not searching through a menu.

Zylo products are designed around immediacy: hands-on controls, clear layouts, and visual systems that make modulation feel alive. The goal is simple: help you move from a quiet idea to a finished texture without losing the spark.

Every instrument is built for the parts of music production that are hard to fake: happy accidents, physical interaction, evolving movement, and the strange little moments that make a sound feel personal.

Principles

01

Make motion playable.

XY fields, modulation trails, sequencers, and performance controls are treated as musical surfaces, not hidden utilities.

02

Keep depth visible.

Deep synthesis and effects matter, but the layout should invite exploration instead of forcing a manual into the session.

03

Protect the atmosphere.

The interface should stay quiet enough for long sessions while still feeling like an object with character and presence.

Made For

  • Producers building melodic ideas from texture and movement.
  • Sound designers shaping evolving pads, basses, keys, drones, and effects.
  • Experimental musicians who want instruments that react like systems.
  • Creators sketching late at night with a keyboard, headphones, and one good sound.

The Zylo Feeling

Minimal on the surface. Deep under the hands. Built for the moment when a patch starts behaving like a performance.
