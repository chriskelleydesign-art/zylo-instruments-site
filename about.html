About Zylo

Modern audio tools for texture, space, and motion.

Zylo Instruments creates focused plugins for producers and sound designers drawn to atmosphere, modulation, and experimental sound.

Brand Story

Built for the moment when a sound starts becoming an idea.

Zylo is an independent audio instrument project shaped around motion, restraint, and hands-on exploration. The tools are made for musicians who like sound design to feel visual and physical without turning the session into a technical exercise.

Each product starts from a musical behavior: a drone that evolves, a field that can be played, a reverb that becomes part of the note, a delay that behaves like an instrument. The interface stays direct so the strange parts of the sound can stay alive.

What Zylo Makes

Software instruments for motion, texture, and atmosphere.

Texture

Sculpt the infinite hum.

Medrone turns synthesis into evolving tone: drones, pads, basses, bells, and layered movement that keep unfolding after the first note.

Atmosphere

Space is an instrument.

Zyloverb and Zylodelay treat reverb and delay as playable sound sources: rooms, repeats, width, drift, shimmer, and time as texture.

Motion

Sound in perpetual motion.

Orbit Weave and Zylo modulation systems make movement visible and performable through fields, XY gestures, sequencing, and drifting control.

Design Philosophy

Focused, visual, musical.

01

Immediate first.

A Zylo plugin should invite the first gesture quickly. Depth is there, but the first move should feel obvious.

02

Movement is part of the sound.

Modulation is treated as performance material: visible, throw-able, recordable, and satisfying to shape.

03

The interface should disappear.

Controls stay quiet, readable, and close to the instrument so you can stay inside the sound instead of the software.
