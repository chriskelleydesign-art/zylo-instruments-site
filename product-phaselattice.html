About

Phase Lattice is a dimensional phase modulation synthesizer that treats waveforms as structural elements — interlocking, bending, and reshaping one another across a grid of phase relationships. At its core is a six-operator PM engine with freely assignable algorithm routing, wrapped in a spectral display that makes the invisible visible. The result is a synthesis environment capable of both crystalline precision and unpredictable harmonic complexity.

Available as VST3, AU  ·  Windows & Mac  ·  64-bit only

Features

Six-operator phase modulation engine with 32 user-editable algorithm presets

Live spectral display — real-time harmonic visualization of the output signal

Ratio lock mode — keeps operator tunings harmonically related as you transpose

Per-operator velocity and aftertouch routing for expressive dynamics

Eight-stage envelope per operator with looping and sustain loop modes

Integrated wavefolder and bitcrusher for digital edge and harmonic saturation

200+ factory presets across bell, metallic, glass, vocal, and evolving categories

Full MPE support — per-note pitch, pressure, and slide on every operator
